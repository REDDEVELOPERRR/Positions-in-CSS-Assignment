Positioned relative to the nearest positioned ancestor (or the initial containing block if none). Positioned relative to its normal position in the document flow.
Removed from the normal document flow. Other elements may overlap or ignore the absolute-positioned element. Maintains its position in the normal document flow, and other elements respect its space.
Positioned relative to the nearest ancestor with a positioning context (position: relative, position: absolute, position: fixed). Positioned relative to its normal position within its parent element.
May overlap or be overlapped by other elements. Does not overlap or affect the positioning of other elements.
